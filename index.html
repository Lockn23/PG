How InstaPay
Works

01

MERCHANT LISTS PRODUCT

Add your product to the Django admin. A unique QR code is auto-generated for each item in the store.

02

CUSTOMER SCANS QR

Customer scans the QR code with their phone. They enter their mobile number to initiate payment.

03

BEYONIC SENDS REQUEST

InstaPay signals Beyonic API to send a Collection Request to the customer's mobile money wallet.

04

PAYMENT CONFIRMED

Beyonic calls back the webhook. Payment status updates to SUCCESS. Merchant notified instantly.
